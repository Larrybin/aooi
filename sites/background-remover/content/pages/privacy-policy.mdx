# Privacy Policy

Background Remover uses uploaded images only to remove backgrounds and provide transparent PNG results.

## Uploaded Images

Uploaded originals and processed PNG results are stored only as needed to provide the service, account history, downloads, and abuse prevention. They are not used to train AI models.

## Retention

Guest and free-user retention is limited by the active plan. Paid plans may retain processed images longer so users can download previous results.
