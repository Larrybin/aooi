## Common Tabs

Use these tabs to finish the basic integration setup after the app is live.

- `/admin/settings/auth`
- Auth provider and sign-in settings.
- `/admin/settings/payment`
- Billing and payment provider settings.
- `/admin/settings/email`
- Email delivery and sender settings.
- `/admin/settings/general`
- General app-wide settings.
